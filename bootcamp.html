Age Groups & Sessions

6-9 Years: Junior Builders

Perfect for beginners! Kids will learn basic robotics concepts through fun, hands-on activities.

  • Simple robot construction
  • Visual block coding
  • Team collaboration
  • Max 12 students per session

10-14 Years: Tech Masters

Advanced program for kids ready to dive deeper into robotics and programming.

  • Advanced robot design
  • Python & Scratch coding
  • Sensor integration
  • Max 10 students per session

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cost of the bootcamp?

Please contact us for current pricing and any available discounts for multiple weeks or siblings.

Does my child need prior experience?

No prior experience is required! We welcome beginners and adjust instruction based on each child's skill level.

What is your cancellation policy?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the session start date. After that, partial refunds may be available.

What is the instructor-to-student ratio?

We maintain a 1:6 instructor-to-student ratio to ensure personalized attention for each child.

Can my child attend multiple weeks?

Yes! Each week builds on previous concepts, so attending multiple weeks enhances the learning experience.

What safety measures are in place?

All equipment is child-safe, and we have certified first-aid staff on-site. We follow strict safety protocols.

Will my child take home a robot?

While the advanced robotics kits remain at the center, children will create projects and receive digital portfolios of their work.

Do you offer any scholarships or financial aid?

Yes, we offer limited scholarships based on financial need. Please contact us for application details.
